About Patson Enterprises

We are Kenya's trusted marketplace for quality new and second-hand goods

Patson Enterprises has been serving customers across Kenya for years, specializing in high-quality furniture, vehicles, land, motorbikes, electronics, and home appliances. Every item we sell is carefully inspected and verified to ensure you get the best value for your money.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with us

How do I place an order?
You can add items to cart and contact us via WhatsApp or phone to confirm and arrange payment/delivery.
Do you offer warranties?
Yes! Most items come with a warranty (3–12 months). Details are provided on each product.
Do you deliver nationwide?
Yes, we deliver to all counties in Kenya. Charges vary by location and item size.
What payment methods do you accept?
M-Pesa, bank transfer, cash on delivery (smaller items), and installments on selected products.
